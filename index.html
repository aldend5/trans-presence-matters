Trans Presence Matters

Trans presence is not new.
What is missing is how often it is seen, heard, and understood.
Things are shifting — in language, in policy, in everyday life.
What is felt does not always match what is written.
This space exists to pay attention.
To understand, slowly, how and why these changes land on real lives.
If you are part of the trans community,
speak in your own terms — as it is lived.
If you are here to listen,
stay with what is unfamiliar.
Learn without needing to conclude.
Speak if you want to.
Or read until you realise you have to.
