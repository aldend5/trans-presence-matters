Trans Presence Matters

We are already here.

Not newly. Not suddenly.

Just more visibly. More vulnerably.

There are changes unfolding — in language, in policy, in how lives are being defined from the outside.

But what is being felt does not always survive how it is written.

And what is written does not always reflect how it lands.

This space exists to hold that difference.

To make room for what is being experienced — not summarised.

To understand, slowly and honestly, how and why things are affecting real lives.

If you are part of the trans community, this is a place to speak in your own terms.

Not to perform clarity. Not to simplify yourself. Not to make it easier for others.

Just to say what is true — as it is being lived.

If you are here to listen, stay with what is uncomfortable.

Read without turning experience into takeaway.

Learn without needing to conclude.

Speak if you want to.
Or read until you realise you have to.
