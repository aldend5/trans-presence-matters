Trans Presence Matters

Trans presence is not new.
This is a space where it becomes more visible, more heard, more understood.

There are shifts happening that are being felt deeply —
not always reflected in how they are written or spoken about.

This space exists to stay with that reality.
To understand, slowly and honestly, how and why it is being lived.

If you are part of the trans community,
you can share what is true — in your own words, on your own terms.

If you are here to listen,
read with care. Stay with what you don’t immediately understand.

Speak if you want to.
Or read until you realise you have to.
Enter
This space is held by an ally, and shaped by those who choose to speak within it.
