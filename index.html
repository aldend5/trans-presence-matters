Trans Presence Matters

Trans presence is not new. This is where it becomes more visible.

Things are shifting — in language, in policy, in everyday life. What is felt is not always what is understood.

This space exists to listen, to hold, and to slowly understand how and why that gap exists.

If you are part of the trans community, this is a place to speak — in your own terms, at your own pace.

If you are here to listen, stay with it. Read without turning experience into takeaway.
