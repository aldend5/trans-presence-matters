About

This space was built out of frustration.

Decisions were made without trans voices being meaningfully included. This is an attempt to shift that — not by speaking for anyone, but by creating space where people can speak for themselves.

If you're here to learn, you're welcome. If you're here to listen, even better.

Moderation

Submissions are reviewed before being published.

We are not trying to control voices — but we do draw lines around harm.

Anything that denies identity, reduces lived experience to debate, or attacks contributors will not be published.

If something is rejected, the goal is not punishment, but to maintain a space where people feel safe to exist.
