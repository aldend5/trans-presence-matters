I’m building this as someone who is not trans, but who has been feeling the distance between what is being lived and what is being understood.

There is a lot being said around trans lives. Not enough space is being held for what is actually being experienced.

If you are trans, this space is here for you to express what is real for you — without needing to simplify it.

If you are here as an ally, including me, this space asks you to listen without turning what is shared into something to debate or extract from.

Nothing here is meant to represent everyone. It is simply a space for presence.
